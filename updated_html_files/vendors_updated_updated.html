A vendor is any person, business, or entity that offers a service.

For example:

  • A landscaper
  • A locksmith
  • A general contractor

Add a vendor

  1. Go to Accounting menu.
  2. Go to Vendors page.

  3. Click Add vendor.

  4. Complete the form.

    The 1099-MISC tax filing information section allows you to capture a specific name and address that will only be used at tax time when you're generating your 1099s through Buildium.

    Save time! If you think you'll need to file a 1099-MISC at the end of the tax year for this person or business, have a W-9 form (Request for Taxpayer Identification Number and Certification) filled out for you now, instead of waiting until year end.

  5. Click .

Change a vendor

  1. Go to Accounting menu.
  2. Go to Vendors page.
  3. Click the vendor name that you wish to edit.

  4. Click the appropriate Edit button for the section you wish to change.

  5. Make your changes in the edit panel.

    The 1099-MISC tax filing information section allows you to capture a specific name and address that will only be used at tax time when you're generating your 1099s through Buildium.

  6. Click .

Inactivate a vendor

  1. Go to Accounting menu.
  2. Go to Vendors page.
  3. Click on the vendor name that you'd like to inactivate.

  4. Click .

Use this feature to remove a vendor from the list of active vendors and retain the accounting history associated with the vendor.

If you need to work with this vendor again, click Reactivate vendor.

Delete a vendor

  1. Go to Accounting menu.
  2. Go to Vendors page.
  3. Click the Vendor you wish to delete

  4. Click .

  5. Confirm that you really want to delete the vendor in the confirmation screen.

Deleting a vendor completely removes the vendor from your system. You can only delete a vendor if there are no accounting transactions or work orders associated with the vendor.
