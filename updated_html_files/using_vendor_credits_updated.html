Article #: 112300

In Buildium, you can enter and process credits from a vendor. The amount due to the vendor will be reduced by the amount of the credit issued.

After a vendor issues a credit to you, you must enter the credit information in the Buildium system. see "Vendor credits" for more information.

After you have entered a credit, you can then redeem the credit by applying the credit against an outstanding bill. see "Pay bills".
